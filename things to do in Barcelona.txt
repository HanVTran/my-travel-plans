The Park Güell is a public park system composed of gardens and architectonic elements located on 
Carmel Hill, in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Carmel Hill belongs to the mountain range of Collserola – 
the Parc del Carmel is located on the northern face. Wikipedia

Casa Batlló is a building in the center of Barcelona. It was designed by Antoni Gaudí, 
and is considered one of his masterpieces. A remodel of a previously built house, 
it was redesigned in 1904 by Gaudí and has been refurbished several times after that. Wikipedia
